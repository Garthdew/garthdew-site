Flow

← Back to projects

Created entirely with the Roland SH-101 synthesiser and two FX pedals, this album came together in just two weeks, capturing moments of creative flow inspired by a period of self-discovery and experimentation. The music weaves together peaceful soundscapes, playful melodies, textured drones, and hazy arpeggios. Tracks were either performed live in one take, built by layering loops in a generative style, or shaped using the SH-101's onboard arpeggiator.
