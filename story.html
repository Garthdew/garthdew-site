Story

A Creative Studio of One

I’m endlessly curious and love working on projects that make me think, learn, and create.

I’m not here to scale an agency or churn out generic work — I’m here to explore ideas, tell stories, and craft meaningful things through creativity and technology.

The studio is small by design — it’s just me — which keeps things fast, personal, and flexible. Whether it’s a brand film, a digital product, or a new experiment, you work directly with the person who’s hands-on from start to finish.

How I Got Here

Before this, I was a professional rugby player. When injury ended that chapter, I had to start again from scratch. I picked up a camera, taught myself how to tell visual stories, and slowly built a creative career.

That reinvention shaped how I work today — a powerful mix of discipline, creativity, and curiosity:

  • Discipline from years in sport — showing up and doing the work.
  • Creativity from working on original and commercial projects.
  • Curiosity from having to learn every step of the way.

Life Highlights

These achievements inform the drive and commitment I bring to every project:

The Work

Over the years, I’ve worked with brands like Volvo, Jaguar Land Rover, Quorn, Speedo, Arla, Autodesk, Neve, Skiddle, Keen, UCLan, and University of Cumbria — helping them tell stories and connect with people.

Alongside client work, I explore my own projects across film, photography, music, and digital products. I like trying new things, making useful tools, and experimenting in public.

The studio is where all of this converges. You get the creative, the technical, and the entrepreneurial mindset working together to solve your specific challenge.
