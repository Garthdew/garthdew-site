A Creative Studio of One

I’m endlessly curious and love working on projects that make me think, learn, and create.

I’m not here to scale an agency or churn out generic work — I’m here to explore ideas, tell stories, and craft meaningful things through creativity and technology.

The studio is small by design — it’s just me — which keeps things fast, personal, and flexible. Whether it’s a brand film, a content system, or an AI experiment, you work directly with the person who’s hands-on from start to finish.

How I Got Here

Before all this, I was a professional rugby player. When injury ended that chapter, I had to completely start over. I picked up a camera, taught myself how to tell visual stories, and slowly built a creative career.

This initial phase saw me launch a one-person video production company (GD Video). That venture has been a success over the last decade—working with major brands like Volvo, Jaguar Land Rover, Quorn, Speedo, Arla, Autodesk, Neve, Skiddle, Keen, UCLan, and University of Cumbria.

However, I outgrew the 'video company' label. While film remains a core skill, my curiosity pushed me beyond that single format. I was consistently exploring my own projects across photography, ambient music, digital tools, and launching entrepreneurial side ventures.

This led me to realise my true value wasn't just in producing content, but in bringing a diverse, strategic skillset to the table.

The Studio

This is where all of that converges. I bring a powerful mix of discipline, curiosity, and creativity to every challenge:

  • Discipline from years in sport — showing up and doing the work.
  • Creativity from working on diverse original and commercial projects.
  • Curiosity from having to learn every step of the way.

The studio is where you tap into this multifaceted approach. You get the creative, the technical, and the entrepreneurial mindset working together to solve your specific challenge. I like trying new things, making useful tools, and experimenting in public—and I bring that spirit of innovation to your project.
