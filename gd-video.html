GD Video

A Decade of Brand Storytelling and High-Performance Execution

GD Video was the ten-year engine room of my creative career, transitioning me from professional sports to a full-time creative studio. The goal was simple: to be the reliable, trusted partner that delivered high-impact visual stories for global names like **Volvo, Jaguar Land Rover, Quorn, and Arla**. This wasn't just about shooting great video; it was about being handed the creative and strategic reins to execute brand films, case studies, and complex internal comms. This successful decade, where the business consistently hit six-figure years, gave me the foundation of discipline and strategic oversight that still drives the Studio's execution today.

The Challenge: Building Trust at Scale

Major clients needed **high-quality visual content** that could reliably deliver on brand objectives, from internal communication to global product launches. They were looking for a single, dependable partner who could manage both the creative vision and the flawless execution across demanding projects.

The Outcome: Foundation and Future

By focusing on **discipline and strategic insight**, GD Video secured consistent revenue and high-level client trust, successfully proving the ability to handle large-scale storytelling under pressure. This experience now allows **Garth Dew Studio** to treat high-end film and content strategy as a core, reliable service within a much broader creative offering.
