Bio

I am an artist, filmmaker, photographer and musician based in Lancashire, UK.

My personal projects span mediums such as street photography, instrumental music and documentary film, capturing people, places, and moments through careful observation.

I also run GD Video, creating cinematic films for brands including Volvo, Jaguar Land Rover, Quorn, Speedo, Arla, Autodesk, Ellis Brigham, Neve, Skiddle, Keen, UCLan, and University of Cumbria. My films have been featured on the BBC and at Kendal Mountain.

After a brief spell as a professional rugby player, I studied music production before pursuing a creative career and life.
