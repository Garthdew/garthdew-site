Bio

I'm a UK-based multidisciplinary artist working across film, photography and music.

My original projects stem from a fascination with humans and the spaces they inhabit.

My commercial projects tell stories in a raw, cinematic style. Clients include Volvo, Jaguar Land Rover, Quorn, Speedo, Arla, Autodesk, Neve, Skiddle, Keen, UCLan, and University of Cumbria. My films have featured on the BBC and Kendal Mountain Film Festival.

Before pursuing a creative life, I played professional rugby and studied music production.
