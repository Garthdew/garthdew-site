Bio

I'm a multidisciplinary artist living in the UK, working worldwide. My original projects focus on humans and the spaces they inhabit through analog and digital photography, ambient music and film. I also run GD Video, creating commercial films for clients including Volvo, Jaguar Land Rover, Quorn, Speedo, Arla, Autodesk, Neve, Skiddle, Keen, UCLan, and University of Cumbria. My films have featured on the BBC and Kendal Mountain Film Festival. I previously played professional rugby before studying music production and pursuing a creative life.
