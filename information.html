Bio

I'm a sound and visual artist from Lancashire, UK. My projects are an exploration of attention and awareness in the present moment, capturing what I see and feel through photography, sound, film, and language. I also run GD Video, crafting cinematic stories for global brands. Before pursuing a creative life, I played professional rugby and studied music production.
