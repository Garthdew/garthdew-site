Bio

I'm a UK-based multidisciplinary artist working across film, photography and music. I create original projects alongside commercial work for clients including Volvo, Jaguar Land Rover, Quorn, Speedo, Arla, Autodesk, Neve, Skiddle, Keen, UCLan, and University of Cumbria. My films have featured on the BBC and Kendal Mountain Film Festival. Before pursuing a creative life, I played professional rugby and studied music production.
