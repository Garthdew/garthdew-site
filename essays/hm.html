On The Happiest Man

I was on holiday in Corfu.

One day, I went on a hike with a local guide.

We explored caves, private beaches, olive groves and remote villages.

We talked about all sorts - from culture, to food, diet, exercise, parenting and philosophy.

The conversation was easy and enjoyable.

Here's how his life looks:

  • Father of two grown boys.
  • Runs a family business doing hiking, bike tours and home cooking classes with small groups of tourists.
  • Works half days throughout summer.
  • In winter, rides his motorbike through Europe and goes skiing.
  • Grows all his own vegetables, and has 17 olive trees which produce 91kg of extra-virgin olive oil per year.
  • Built his family home in a remote village for the cost of an average UK terrace house.
  • Enjoys a glass of local wine each evening with a home-cooked family meal.
  • Never had more than a mild cold and has an annual MOT which he always passes with flying colours.

He seemed like the happiest man I had ever met..
