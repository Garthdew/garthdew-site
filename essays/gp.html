On Permission

From the day we're born, we live up to the expectations of others.

First, our parents.

Then our friends and teachers.

Then our spouse and employers.

We worry so much about what others think.

It's easy to go through a lifetime without ever living your truth.

One of the most empowering things you can do is to give yourself permission.

To choose your own path.

To do what excites you.

To start.

To quit.

Giving yourself permission is the greatest gift you can give to yourself and the world.

When you follow the heart, you are alive.
