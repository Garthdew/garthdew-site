On Being A Generalist

Dont specialise.

Generalise.

Specialising made sense in bygone times.

But now the world moves too fast.

If I’d only stuck to videography for the last 10 years, I’d now be vulnerable to advances in technology and global competition.

Luckily, I’ve built a skill stack beyond video.

A skill stack is a set of complimentary skills that gives you more options and chances of success.

Through continuous learning, building a business, doing side projects and saying yes to lots of opportunities, I have developed other skills such as:

  • Content marketing
  • Public speaking
  • Photography
  • Psychology
  • Coaching
  • Branding
  • Writing
  • Design
  • Sales
  • AI

Layer these skills on top of my core skill of video production, and I have many options for making a living.

And I don’t expect I’ll ever be short of opportunities in the future.

Generalists will adapt and thrive in the next decade.

Specialists will be replaced by technology or global talent who work for less.
