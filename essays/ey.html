On Embarrassing Yourself

Sometimes I feel bored with my core business.

10 years is a long time to do the same thing week in, week out.

I often feel bad for this.

”You’ve worked hard to get here. Get your head down and push on!”

But the other day, I read a great reframe on this point.

If you’re feeling bored with business or life, you’re not risking embarrassment enough.

We stay stuck where we are, doing the same old things, because we’re scared of embarrassing ourselves doing something new.

For example, I’ve started writing about topics that are different to what I’m known for.

I’m risking embarrassment if my posts flop.

Or if people think, ‘What is Garth on about?”

And writing about topics like health or spirituality, that could seem 'cringe.'

But writing gives me a lot of energy.

I’m excited to work on it.

And I know it will lead somewhere interesting.

Like building new relationships or attracting opportunities.

So if you’re feeling bored, find some ways of potentially embarrassing yourself.

  • Ask someone on a date who is ‘out of your league’.
  • Start a side project around something you’re excited about.
  • Start writing online to attact like minds, even if your friends take the piss.

It will add some zest to your life and you never know where it could lead.
