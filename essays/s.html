On Surrendering

I've noticed something about business and life.

The more you chase something.

Or the more you try to force it.

The more it evades you.

When you relax and surrender.

And accept what will be, will be.

The more the object of desire finds its way to you.

So, take it day by day.

Do the necessary actions–without attachment to outcomes.

Be less needy.

And trust the world.

You will eventually get what you want.
