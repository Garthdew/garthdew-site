On Hunting Antelope

A great question for creative people:

“Are you chasing field mice or hunting antelope?”

Field mice are the little, insignificant projects.

They're easier to catch, but you can't live on field mice.

The antelope is the big, meaningful project.

It's going to be much harder to catch, but the rewards will be huge. You can thrive on antelope.

What is the possibility that would get you stoked about the future?

The thing so big and mind-blowing that you barely dare to think it?

Spend some time pondering that.
