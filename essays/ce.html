On Cold Emailing

If you want to make interesting things happen...

Get in the habit of emailing people you admire or find interesting.

Whether it's an athlete, author, artist, or founder.

Doing this has led to some of my best projects and relationships.

The key is to be genuine and not make huge asks, like "become my mentor" or "buy my services."

Just share what you appreciate about them or their work.

An email like that can make someone's day, no matter how 'big time' they are.

It may lead somewhere, it may not, it's all good.

For me, it's far more effective, and fun, than posting into the social media abyss.

Or cold email blasting businesses you have no interest in whatsoever.

Marketing and sales doesn't have to be sleazy.

It's just about building genuine relationships.

Sometimes the best approach is just being a human.
