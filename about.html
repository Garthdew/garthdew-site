About

I am an artist, filmmaker, photographer & musician based in Lancashire, UK.

My original work stems from a deep curiosity about the human experience and the world around me. I pay attention to people, places, emotions and the creative forces that guide me, capturing what emerges in the way that feels most natural. My practice creates a container for presence - a space where I can cultivate awareness, enter flow, and stay open to the work that wants to come through me.

I also run GD Video, a story-first video production company, where I create cinematic films that help brands and personalities build deeper connections with their audiences.

Get in touch at garth@gdvideo.co.uk.
