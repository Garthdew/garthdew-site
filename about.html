I'm an artist based in Northern England.

I photograph the people I meet, the places I go, and the moments that catch my eye. I also make films and instrumental music. My practice comes from a deep curiosity about the world.

Alongside my own projects, I run GD Video, a commercial film and photography studio working with brands like Volvo, Speedo, Arla, Quorn, and Neve.
