About

I'm Garth Dew. Now In Flow is my independent publication and studio.

I publish stories from people doing their life's work — artists, athletes, founders, and thinkers who care about craft, integrity, and depth.

The publication features photo essays, films, conversations, and experiments. Some emerge quickly. Others unfold over months or years.

Print is central to the project. Selected work is collected into limited-run magazines and objects.

Flowing outside trends, schedules, and algorithms, this is a container for the work — made when ready, not on demand.
