Business

I am planning an experience to help others reconnect with their inner artist and publish an original body of work. The first edition will be around photography, with the aim of publishing a group zine at the conclusion.

Original Art

I've been creating original art over the last 12 months, for the first time since I was at university. I am going between music and photography primarily. It's such a joy to create for it's own sake again.

Coaching

I now feel like I'm coming home to my self, and living in harmony with my true nature.
